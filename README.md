# Smart Legal Aid & Awareness System

This project is developed as part of an online hackathon.

## Problem Statement
Many people lack basic legal awareness and do not know what action to take for common legal or social issues.

## Solution
The Smart Legal Aid & Awareness System provides a simple digital platform where users can submit their issues and receive suggested legal actions along with relevant resources and helpline information.

## Features
- Simple issue reporting using Google Forms
- Automated suggested actions
- Relevant legal resources and helplines
- User-friendly interface

## Google Technologies Used
- Google Forms
- Google Sheets
- Google Slides
- Google Drive
